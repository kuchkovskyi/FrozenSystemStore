What's with my order?

Want to know what stage your order is at? Track his status in your account.

What does order status mean?
  • New order — we received your application and took it to work.
  • Processed by the manager — the goods are reserved at the warehouse and prepared for delivery.
  • Automatically processed — this status means that we will not bother you with a call to confirm the order. Your order is being prepared.
  • It is completed with a carrier — the carrier took the order and prepares it for dispatch.
  • Should be in the recipient city — your order is on the way.
  • Awaiting customer at the pickup point— the order is ready for delivery, and you can pick it up.
  • Waiting for a customer at the post office — the order is ready for delivery, you can pick it up from the post office.
  • Located in the city of the recipient — the order has arrived in your city and is waiting at the pick-up point. Shelf life is three business days.
  • The reserve term ends today — this status means that the reserve term is expiring. Hurry to pick up the order!

How to pay by card?

You can pay for your order by card - online or upon receipt in our store. To pay by card in the store: when placing an order, select a payment method Payment upon receipt of order.

To pay for your order online:

  1. Add the product to the basket and proceed to checkout.
  2. Choose a payment method Pay now with Visa/MasterCard.
  3. Choose exactly how you want to pay: via Masterpass or online card.
  4. Complete checkout.
  5. Next, you will go to the secure payment system page where you can confirm the payment.

You can also pay by card with Google Pay or PrivatPay.

Please note: to pick up an order paid for online, you must provide an identity document.

How to buy goods on credit?

You can buy on credit goods from the seller FrozenSystem and goods from other sellers.
The minimum order amount for payment on credit is 50 $ ..

How to buy goods on credit?
  1. Go to the product page.
  2. To view available loan packages, click Learn details in the loan block.
  3. When placing an order, select the method of payment Credit, the desired loan package and the number of payments.
  4. Fill in all the data and click Confirm Order.
  5. Next, expect a call from the bank and draw up a contract.

You can choose a convenient address for signing the contract, get acquainted with the loan conditions and bank licenses.

How to cancel the order?

You can cancel the order if you selected payment upon receipt.

  1. Go to the My Orders section.
  2. Click on an order to see more information.
  3. Click Cancel Order on the upper right of the order block.
  4. Check the box ( ) to confirm the cancellation of the order.
  5. After that, the "Cancel this order" button will become active.
  6. Click it. That's it - you have successfully canceled the order. :)

How do I know if my order can already be picked up?

How do you know if your order is ready for delivery?

  1. Go to My Orders.
  2. Select your order.

You can pick up an order if its status is one of the following:

  • Waiting for a client at the post office (reserve - 2 days from the moment of posting at the post office).
  • It is located in the city of the recipient (storage period is three working days).
  • Waiting for the customer at the pick-up point (reserve - 3 days, including the day of the planned delivery. If you do not have time to pick up the order by this time, you can extend the reserve).
